Vanilla Protein Shake

This vanilla protein shake is perfect for drinking after a heavy workout. It will help your muscles to recover.

Ingredients:

  • 6 oz of water.
  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder.
  • Ice cubes (optional).

Preparation:

  1. Fill a glass with the 6 oz of water
  2. Add the ice cubes (optional).
  3. Add the protein powder to the glass.
  4. Stir until the powder dissolves.
  5. Enjoy it!
